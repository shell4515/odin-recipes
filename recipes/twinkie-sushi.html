Twinkie Sushi

Description

Once upon a time, I wanted to make sushi, but I had no rice, seaweed, or anything else required to make this delicious treat. What did I have on hand? Twinkies. Lots of twinkies and green fruit leather. Bingo, twinkie sushi was born. It was a labor of love. Everyone thought I was crazy, and I was/am. I don't really have much else to say about why I would create such an abomination for human consumption.

Ingredients

  • 3 Hostess Twinkies
  • assorted dried fruits
  • assorted fruity decorative candies
  • 2 green fruit leather
  • dried mango (looks like pickled ginger)

Steps

  1. Slice Twinkies into pieces about an inch tall.
  2. Slice fruit rollups in strips to be long enough and wide enough to wrap around the Twinkie pieces.
  3. Place dried fruits and candies into the cream filling.
  4. Place Twinkie rolls on a plate or in a Bento box.
  5. Garnish with strips of dried mango to resemble pickled ginger if you wish.
  6. Serve with chopsticks.
